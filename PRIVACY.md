# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

StudyHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

StudyHub stores all user data locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes:

- User profile information (name, university, department)
- Course data and notes
- Habit tracking data
- Grade calculations
- Schedule information
- Application preferences and settings

### Data We Do NOT Collect

StudyHub does NOT:
- Collect personal data on external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use cookies for tracking
- Require user registration or accounts
- Access your device's contacts, location, or other personal information

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your web browser's localStorage. This means:

- Your data never leaves your device
- Your data is not transmitted over the internet
- Your data is only accessible to you on your device
- Clearing browser data will remove all stored information

## Third-Party Services

StudyHub uses the following external resources:

- **Google Fonts** - For loading the Inter font family
- **Google Actions Audio** - For Pomodoro timer notification sounds

These services may have their own privacy policies.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on:

- Your device's security measures
- Your browser's security settings
- Whether others have access to your device

We recommend:
- Using a secure device
- Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals
- Regularly backing up important data

## Data Backup and Export

You can export your data from the Settings page. This creates a backup file that you can save and restore later.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your StudyHub data:

1. Go to Settings
2. Select "Logout" or "Reset"
3. Confirm the action

Alternatively, you can clear your browser's localStorage for this site.

## Children's Privacy

StudyHub is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our repository's issue tracker.
